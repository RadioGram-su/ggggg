Gram Radar DNS Bot (@gramradardns_bot)
======================================

Alerts are FREE — link wallet on site, no subscription.

Deploy site files:
  lib/bot-api.js
  lib/auction-watcher.js
  server.js
  public/index.html
  public/js/i18n.js
  public/js/premium-ui.js

Deploy bot files:
  src/bot.js
  src/watcher.js

Then:
  VPS: cd /var/www/gramradar && bash fix.sh
  bothost: Restart bot

Env bothost:
  TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN=
  BOT_API_SECRET= (same as site .env)
  GRAMRADAR_API=https://gramradar.org
  SITE_URL=https://gramradar.org

Env site:
  BOT_API_SECRET=
  EXTERNAL_TELEGRAM_BOT=1
  (no TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN on site)
